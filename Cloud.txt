5 essential characteristics:
    1. On-demand self-service: access cloud resources whenever required
    2. Broad Network Access: cloud computing resources can be accessed through the network
    3. Resource Pooling: consumer saves cost through shared resources
    4. Rapid Elasticity: increase or decrease resources as per demand
        scaled up in vertical scaling or scaled out in horizontal scaling
    5. Measured Service: pay for only what you use or reserve
        utility model of billing where you are charged after the usage
